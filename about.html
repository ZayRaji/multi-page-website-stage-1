About ZR

Bio

Hello there! I am Zainab Raji. I am a Frontend Developer on a journey toward becoming a Fullstack Developer. My mission is to create inclusive and accessible systems, one line of code at a time. I believe that technology should be available to everyone, regardless of their abilities or the devices they use.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading tech articles, teaching others what I have learned, or writing articles. I'm constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and skills, and I love sharing that journey with others in the developer community.

My Goals in This Program

  • Master modern web development technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript,REACT and popular frameworks
  • Build a strong foundation in responsive design and accessibility best practices
  • Create a portfolio of real-world projects that showcase my problem-solving abilities
  • Learn backend development and database management to become a true fullstack developer
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code that other developers can easily understand
  • Develop the confidence to tackle complex programming challenges independently
  • Connect with other developers and build a strong professional network in the tech community

Areas Where I Need to Build Confidence

  • Backend development and server-side technologies - This is still relatively new territory for me
  • Working with complex APIs and handling asynchronous operations effectively
  • Database design and management - Understanding when to use SQL vs NoSQL solutions
  • Debugging complex issues systematically - I need to develop more structured approaches to troubleshooting
  • Performance optimisation and writing efficient algorithms that scale well
  • State management in larger applications - Understanding best practices for React, Vue, or other frameworks
  • Speaking confidently about my technical skills in interviews and professional networking situations
